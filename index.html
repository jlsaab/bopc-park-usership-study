About This Map

The Buffalo Olmsted Parks Conservancy (BOPC) tasked an NYU Wagner Capstone student team with designing a comprehensive park usership method. This map shows the recommended observation points and routes that should be studied over four seasons to get enough samples to generate an accurate estimate of total annual usership. This map is meant to be used by BOPC staff and volunteers to make the data collection more handy with a smartphone.

Map designed by Jessica Saab

Study designed by Jake Mericle, Jessica Saab, Emily Speelman, and Stephen Zubrycky from NYU Wagner Class of 2024
