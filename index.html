How many people use Buffalo's Olmsted Parks every year?

BOPC, the Buffalo Olmsted Parks Conservancy, tasked an NYU Wagner Capstone team with designing a comprehensive park usership method. This map contains the recommended observation points and routes to be studied over four seasons to get an accurate estimate of total annual usership.
